About US

Chalkboards is a smarter point of sale that connects the brick-and-mortar business to the cyber world via its advanced functions. Customers can ‘check-in’ directly with the small business, for loyalty rewards, personalized offers and more. The business owner can send instant promotions by email or text, with just a few taps. With all the powerful built-in tools, a small business owner can save money (using self-service), reduce waste (with instant promotions) and receive mobile orders directly into his point-of-sale. The marketing tools are easy to use, with simple drag-and-drop user interfaces. The business can deploy multiple stations placed directly in the kitchen or warehouse for order-monitoring and to print order tickets at the right place. Using the Chalkboards point-of-sale, businesses can sell more, save more and connect more, and most importantly, use technology like the big corporations, and still maintain his individuality.
