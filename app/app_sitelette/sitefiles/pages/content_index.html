"Where is my Technology?"

Can modern technology help traditional brick-and-mortar small businesses?

Increase sales and save money with a smarter point of sale. Reach your customers the modern way, on their smartphones, directly, from your point-of-sale.
"Free up staff with a self-service Kiosk"

You want to add an extra sales counter without hiring an extra employee? Simply use the built-in self-service kiosk. The kiosk doesn't take a break, never calls in sick. It takes all forms of credit cards, it prints receipts/tickets, and alerts you to new orders in multiple ways. The big corporations are already using self-service, now you can too.

"Reduce waste, on slow day, with instant promos?"

No need to pay some third-party middleman lots of money to send your email and text promotions. You can pass on the savings to your customers. With the Chalkboards POS, you can send promotions to your customers directly, straight from your POS. With a few taps create an instant promotion in seconds, like half-price after 8:30pm, or 20% off when it is raining. They can share the deals on social media, pay via mobile, redeem in-store, right at the point-of-sale. Fully integrated, automatic, no staff training needed.

"Create and send personalized deals to my customers?"

You know who your best customers are. Or who could become a best customer. Now you send them personalized promotions, that only they can use, maybe to remind them to come back and visit you. Or there is some occasion in their life. Chalkboards makes it super easy. All you need is their email or mobile number.

"How about a built-in loyalty program?"

You want to increase repeat visits? Loyalty programs are a time-tested way. Chalkboards makes it easy. Just pick an item, set the rules and activate the built-in Loyalty manager. The Check-in system will do the rest. It will make sure qualifying purchases are counted, awards are given, and will even send out reminder emails if needed. Your customers don’t have to carry a card, or install an app or even sign-up. Just send them an email and the link to their digital card is embedded in the email.

"Get orders from mobiles directly?"

Do you have to keep an eye on multiple iPads from third parties in-case you are getting a mobile order? And do you have to copy orders over to your system, at the end of the day? Wouldn't it be nice if everything was streamlined and mobile orders came through the same point-of-sale, printing tickets for your kitchen like your normal orders? The Chalkboards POS comes with its matching mobile ordering link that is embeded it in all receipts and emails.
