Save costs with Self-service
  • Don't have enough staff to man all stations? Switch front-screen of the Dual-Screen units to self-service with the touch of a button.

  • Deploy with confidence. If an item is out of stock, deactivate across all stations with a touch of button, remotely, no need for restart or reboot.
