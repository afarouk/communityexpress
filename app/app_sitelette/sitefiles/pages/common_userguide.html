User Guide

Manage your business

You can manage your Mobile App either from Dashboard in this website or from the iPad Dashboard App.
  1. The HTML5 'Mobile App' you have created is stored on our cloud servers.
  2. The Sitelettes Dashboard App lets you modify your 'Mobile App' content.
  3. For this reason, you must have good Internet Access (3G/WiFi etc).
  4. Some of your uploads may require "Approval", it will depend on your service plan.
  5. Any updates you make are instantly available to anyone using your 'Mobile App'.
  6. Note that in the current version, you cannot change the layout, but can only change the content.
In the following sections we will show you how to carry out specific functions with your Sitelettes Dashboard App.

Manage your pictures

  1. To add a new picture, click/tap on the 'Add New' button.
  2. After you have uploaded picture, look at the status indicator. If it is 'Approved', only then you can 'Activate' the picture.
  3. You can drag a picture around pressing and dragging on the little drag thumb. The middle column indicates your currently Active pictures. While the rows on the left show In-Active pictures. Only active pictures are visible on the Mobile App.
  4. If your Mobile App users take pictures and upload them, you will them in the 'User Pictures' row. You can activate/deactivate them the say way.
  5. You can update the title and description of any picture at any time.
  6. If you need a reminder about which button does what, click on the 'i' on the top right of the screen.

Add a picture

When you tap on the 'Add New' button, a new view appears. This is the picture composer/upload view.
  1. Tap on the 'camera-upload' button and select if you want to upload an existing image or take a new one.
  2. Your picture will be cropped around the blue mask. You can choose either portrait or landscape. pinch/zoom and drag to get the correct content in the cropping mask.
  3. Title: Fill in a suitable title for picture.
  4. Description: Write a few words of description, or some message.
  5. When ready, click the 'Submit' button, your picture will be uploaded and cropped as you chose.
  6. If you need a reminder about which button does what, click on the 'i' on the top right of the screen.

Edit Promotions

Promotions are special form of media that are highlighted in your Mobile App.
  1. To add a new promotions, click on the Add New Promotion button.
  2. Once you have successfully added a promotion, it is IN-Active. You have to Activate a promotion before your fans can see it. However, you can still Edit a promotion while it is In-Active. You cannot edit a promotion once it is Active.

Add a new promotion

When you click on the Add New Promotion button, you navigate to this screen.
  1. First, select a picture. You have to select from the available approved pictures.
  2. If you want to a new picture, click on the Add New Picture button. Remember, depending on your service contract, your picture may have to be Approved. All your pictures will show up on the top row.
  3. Next, pick a 'type' for your Promotion. This is important as it will determine how your promotion is highlighted on our 'Near Me' Maps.
  4. Enter a title for Promotion. Titles should be short and clear
  5. Enter some description for your promotion. Include conditions, price etc, ... any details your fans would want to know.
  6. When ready, click Submit. Your promotion will be uploaded. Note that if your 'picture' is new, it might need to be Approved
What's the difference between a Promotion and Picture? Picture are not given unique ids, while Promotions all have unique IDs and can thus be used for reservations, record keeping etc.

update app icon

The 'App Icon' is the picture that is placed on the Home Screen of a smartphone when your fans bookmark your Mobile App.
  1. Take a new picture or select a picture for your App Icon. Make sure the Square Mask shows the area you want to use.
  2. When ready, click the Submit button. Remember, your icon may need to be Approved, depending on your service contract.
  3. Once Approved, your fans will see their App Icon change when they touch it (this actually depends on the smartphone version, model etc, but in general, this is the expected behavior).
